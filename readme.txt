Ni WooCommerce Sales Report Email plugin provides periodic email reports with essential sales data, order status, and top-selling order insights

== Description ==


<p>
The <strong>Ni WooCommerce Sales Report Email</strong> plugin sends order status email reports at specific time intervals: every 15 minutes, every 6 hours, every 12 hours, and every 24 hours.
</p>

<p>
The plugin's dashboards provide a comprehensive sales analysis report, showcasing the total number of orders placed, sales for the current month and year, and other essential data to boost your online sales.
</p>

<p>
Furthermore, this report features visually appealing graphs representing order status and payment gateway sales reports, along with a detailed breakdown of the top ten highest-selling orders.
</p>


<h4>WooCommerce Sales Report Email Feature</h4>
<ul>
<li>Dashboard Summary Report - Total Orders placed, Order Total, Discounts in Total, Total Product Sold</li>
<li>Ability to send the sales reports in email.</li>
<li>Provide setting option to set automated email received interval for every 15 minute, every day etc.</li>
<li>Provide setting option to set To email address, from email address and subject line.</li>
<li>Enable/disable automated email notification.</li>
<li>Filter option - Today sales, Yesterday sales, Last 7 days sales, Last 30 days sales, This year sales</li>
<li>Show top 10 recent order list</li>
<li>Show graphical/tabular order stauts report</li>
<li>Show graphical/tabular payment gateway wise sales report</li>
<li>Show total product sold report in email</li>
<li>Added cron job url</li>
</ul>
